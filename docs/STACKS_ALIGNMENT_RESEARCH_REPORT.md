- **Evidence:** Strong (multiple RCTs, meta-analyses)
- **Pregnancy Improvement:** 26-36% better outcomes
- **Recommended Dosage:** 400mg standardized extract (0.6% agnuside)

- **Missing From:** Reproductive Health Foundation, Female Hormone & Fertility

#### 2. **Tribulus Terrestris** - CRITICAL MISSING
- **Evidence:** Strong (meta-analysis 2023, 133 subjects)
- **Sperm Improvements:** +24% concentration, +45% motility
- **Recommended Dosage:** 750-1500mg daily (40-60% saponins)
- **Timing:** Divided doses with meals
- **Timeframe:** 3-6 months (full sperm cycle)
- **Missing From:** Reproductive Health Foundation, Male Libido & Fertility
- **Clinical Support:** Multiple RCTs, in vitro validation

#### 3. **Myo-Inositol** - CRITICAL MISSING (PCOS)
- **Evidence:** Strong (multiple RCTs, meta-analyses)
- **PCOS Ovulation Restoration:** 88% success rate
- **Pregnancy Rate:** 40-72% in PCOS cohorts
- **Recommended Dosage:** 2-4g daily
- **Timeframe:** 3-6 months
- **Missing From:** Female Hormone & Fertility (especially PCOS subset)
- **Clinical Support:** 2024 studies show 58.3% ovulation, 65.3% cycle regularization
