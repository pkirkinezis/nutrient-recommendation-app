## EVIDENCE SUMMARY

### Vitex (Chasteberry)
- **Meta-analysis Results:** 26-36% fertility improvement
- **Key Study:** 96 women with fertility disorders; pregnancy rates significantly higher
- **Mechanism:** Modulates LH/FSH, increases progesterone, normalizes luteal phase
- **Safety:** Well-tolerated; discontinue at conception
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months for full effect

### Tribulus Terrestris
- **Meta-analysis (2023):** 133 subjects
  - Sperm concentration: SDM = 0.624 (improvement)
  - Sperm motility: SDM = 0.742 (improvement)
- **In Vitro:** 40-50 ¬µg/mL enhanced motility and viability
- **Mechanism:** Calcium-mediated cyclic AMP enhancement
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months (full sperm cycle = 74 days)
- **Safety:** Excellent; safe throughout

### Myo-Inositol
- **Key Finding:** 88% restore spontaneous ovulation in PCOS
- **2024 Study:** 58.3% ovulation, 65.3% cycle regularization
- **Pregnancy Rate:** 40-72% in PCOS cohorts
- **Mechanism:** Improves insulin sensitivity (core PCOS driver)
- **Timeline:** 3-6 months for ovulation restoration
- **Safety:** Excellent; minimal GI upset at high doses
